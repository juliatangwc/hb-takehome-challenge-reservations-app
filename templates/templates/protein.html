Diversify Your Protein

Protein is very important for the function and repair of cells.

It can be divided by the sources into animal (red and white meat) and plant-based protein.

Red Meat White Meat Plant-based Protein
  • Pork
  • Beef
  • Lamb
  • Chicken and other poultry
  • Fish and other seafood
  • Soy and products (such as tofu)
  • Other legumes (such as black beans and chickpeas)
  • Nuts (such as almonds and peanuts)

Men only needs about 56g of protein per day while women only needs about 46g of protein per day. Without over avoidance, obtaining sufficient protein from a normal diet is very easy.

On the other hand, consuming too much red and processed meat (such as sausages, ham, canned meat) will increase the risk of some cancers.
